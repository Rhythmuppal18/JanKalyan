About the Portal

Who We Are

A centralised digital platform bridging citizens and government welfare schemes across India.

Our Mission

Empowering Citizens Through Information

JanKalyan is a digital initiative dedicated to bridging the information gap between public welfare programs and the citizens who need them most. We believe that awareness is the first step toward empowerment. Our platform serves as a structured, reliable, and easy-to-use resource designed to bring essential government schemes in housing, healthcare, education, and financial assistance directly to the fingertips of the underserved.

JanKalyan is built on a foundation of transparency, inclusivity, and social responsibility. While our current platform focuses on intuitive front-end delivery, we are committed to evolving

At JanKalyan, we leverage technology not just for innovation, but for impact.
