Eligibility Criteria Overview

This table provides a quick reference for the key eligibility criteria across major government welfare schemes.

Scheme Name Category Income Limit Class/Age Status
PM Scholarship Scheme Student No specific income limit mentioned in official guidelines for this category. Pursuing First Professional Degree (BE, MBBS, etc.) Open
NMMS Scholarship Student Below ₹3.5L/year Class 9–12 Open
Ayushman Bharat (PMJAY) Health Generally monthly income below ₹10,000 for rural All Ages (All 70+ now covered regardless of income) Open
Janani Suraksha Yojana Health BPL (or SC/ST) Pregnant Women Open
PMAY-Gramin Housing Up to ₹15,000/month 18+ Open
PMAY-Urban 2.0 Housing EWS < ₹3L | LIG ₹3–6L | MIG ₹6–9L Adult (18+) Open (CLSS replaced by ISS)
MGNREGS Services Rural Households 18+ Open
PM Ujjwala Yojana Services BPL Adult Women Open
Atal Pension Yojana Services All Rural Households 18–40 Active
PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Services All Landholding Farmers 18+ Open
Pragati Scholarship Student Below ₹8L/year Girl Students (Degree/Dip) Open
Jal Jeevan Mission Services All Rural Households All Ages Ongoing

Note: Eligibility criteria may vary by state and are subject to periodic revision. Always verify eligibility directly with the concerned ministry or CSC (Common Service Centre) before applying.
