Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Why use JanKalyan over visiting government sites directly?

JanKalyan aggregates 480+ schemes from dozens of ministries in one verified interface. Instead of navigating 20+ government portals, you get everything — eligibility criteria, documents needed, and direct apply links — in one place.

How do I check my eligibility for a specific scheme?

Use the Eligibility page for a quick-reference table across all major schemes. The Smart Search above also lets you filter by category. For AI-powered matching, our Pro plan (launching Q2 2026) will match your exact profile to every applicable scheme.

What documents are typically required?

Most schemes require: Aadhaar Card, Income Certificate (from Tehsildar/SDO), BPL/Ration Card, Bank Passbook (for DBT), and a Caste Certificate if applicable. Each scheme's detail modal on this portal lists the exact documents needed.

Can I apply for multiple schemes simultaneously?

Yes. As long as you meet each scheme's individual eligibility criteria, there is no restriction on benefiting from multiple programmes. Many citizens receive benefits from PMAY, Ayushman Bharat, and NMMS concurrently.

Is there a helpline if I get stuck in the application process?

Yes. Visit your nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) or call the Citizen Help Desk: 1800-11-0001. JanKalyan Pro subscribers also get priority email support from our team.
